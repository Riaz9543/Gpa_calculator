How HSC GPA is Calculated

  • All compulsory subjects (Bangla, English, ICT) must be passed
  • At least 3 group subjects must be passed
  • Optional subjects can add bonus points if grade > 2.00
  • Maximum GPA is 5.00
  • Failing any compulsory subject results in GPA 0.00
